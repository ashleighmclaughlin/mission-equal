OUR MISSION:

Gender Equality.

Gender Equality is achieved once women and men can enjoy the same rights and opportunities. Mission Equal is the leading Gender Equal Movement in Aoteroa, fighting against the inequality with live around. Join us to make a difference in our world.

ABOUT

WHO ARE WE?

Mission Equal emerged in 1896 after the successful women's suffrage campaign. We are the leading oganisation fighting for gender eqaulity rights in New Zealand. We bring together those willing to fight for our rights and make a difference in the country we live in. We help pretect those who may suffer from discrimination and diversity in daily life. Join us to make equality and reality in Aoteroa!

SUCCESSES

Since founding the organisation in 1896, we have come a long way in the equality awareness movement. We have been so empowered ever since winning the womens suffrage campaign. We have currently gained an amazing total of $5,500,000 from our generous donaters, which we have put towards creating a safe and welcome environment for those affected by inequality in Aoteroa. We have over 3000 volunteers who help out weekly in running / creating events and campaigns, and raising awareness about this important organisation and community.

WHAT WE DO

Our amazing 5000 volunteres, along with the team at Mission Equal have many events that we get together for to make a difference in this cause. The Mission Equal team meets weekly to discuss new campaigns and movements that we believe will benefit the organisation and community. We create events that people can come along to, to make a difference in our country, and raise awareness about the inequality we live in. We work closely with the government to help get our voice heard and raise awareness.

GOALS FROM HERE

PAY GAP

A controversial topic for some. We aim to break the gender pay gap in Aoteroa and bring equality for women in the work force helping to make a difference in our world. We want women to feel like their work is worth as much as a males work.

WOMEN IN TECH

We aim to break the male stigma around those working in the technology fields in Aoteroa. While more and more are getting interested in field, we want women to feel comfortable and supported in these careers.

FEMALE REPRESENTATION

We are currently raising awareness about needing more female representatives in the Government and court sectors. The female voice should be heard as much as the male voice so we want to break this gap and see more female faces fighting for our country.
